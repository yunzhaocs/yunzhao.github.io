Biography

Dr. Yun Zhao is a research scientist at Meta Platforms, Inc. (formerly known as Facebook, Inc.) at Menlo Park, California, United States. He received his PhD degree in the Department of Computer Science, University of California, Santa Barbara in April, 2021. His Ph.D. advisor is Prof. Linda Petzold. Before that, he received his B.E. degree and M.E. degree from Zhejiang University and Tsinghua University, respectively. His Masters academic advisor is Prof. Zhisheng Niu. He was the recipient of the Best Paper Award in the 9th Workshop on Data Mining in Biomedical Informatics and Healthcare (DMBIH’21). His research interests include machine learning and data mining.
